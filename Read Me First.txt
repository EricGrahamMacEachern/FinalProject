To navigate this site, begin from main.html and follow the links to the other parts of the site. 

Each geological age page (carboniferous.html, eocene.html, holocene.html etc.) links back to the main page. 

From the main page, you can reach every other page on the site, and every page on the site links to the main page. 

There are external inks to berkely.edu on the periods pages that do not link back to the site.
