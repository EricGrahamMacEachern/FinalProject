Paleocene

The Paleocene is a geologic epoch that lasted from 65.5 ± 0.3 million years ago (mya) to 55.8 ± 0.2 mya. It is the first epoch of the Paleogene period in the modern Cenozoic era, and is followed by the Eocene. As with most other older periods of the geologic time scale, the strata that define the Paleocene epoch's beginning and end are well identified, but the exact dates are uncertain.
